Press conference


31th August 2002

While the Seefelder sport facilities and the Kurpark offered varied entertainment shows, the official press conference of the Third World Health & Sports Promotion was held in the WM-Halle. Numerous journalist came to learn more about the work of vita-life® WHSF and this charity event. The press conference with Henry Maske, Silvano Beltrametti, Prof. Dr. Manfred Walzl, the painter Knud Tiroch and Mario Hintermayer revealed details about the global work of vita-life® WHSF.
